The Eco Materials Library is a physical and digital resource for exploring sustainable and emerging materials through samples, recipes, process documentation, and selected tools. It supports material literacy, experimentation, and sustainability learning through direct engagement. The library helps students and researchers inspect materials, understand how they are made, how they behave, and how they can be worked with in practice.
