BOULDER COUNTY FILM COMMISSION

Title: Boulder County Film Commission
2025

Project: Magazine cover design for print promoting Mike's Camera as a source for videography and content creation in Colorado and California.

About: Boulder County Film Commission "offers accessible and high-impact ways to showcase craft and reach new clients, collaborators, and opportunities." With the Sundance Film Festival coming to Boulder, I had the opportunity to design one of the covers for this publication of of the Boulder Film Commission magazine, highlighting creatives and businesses in the industry.
